Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Step 1: Upload Your Data

    To begin, navigate to the Upload Your Data section on the main page. Click on the Choose File button and select the files you want to upload.

  2. Step 2: Select Data Parameters

    Once your data is uploaded, go to the Select ids/species/columns tab. Here, you can specify the parameters for your data.

  3. Step 3: Display Options

    After setting the parameters, click on the Display options tab to customize how you want your data to be displayed.

  4. Step 4: View the Heatmap

    Finally, navigate to the Heatmap image tab to view the heatmap of your data. Adjust the settings as needed to visualize your data effectively.

  5. Step 5: Provide Feedback

    If you have any feedback or encounter issues, please use the Feedback tab to submit your comments and suggestions.
